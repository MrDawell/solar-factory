Frequently Asked Questions

How much subsidy can I get? expand_more

Under the PM-Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, residential consumers can get up to ₹78,000 subsidy for a 3kW system. We handle all the paperwork for you.

What is the cost of installation? expand_more

Costs vary based on system size and panel quality. Typically, a 3kW system starts from ₹1.5 Lakhs (before subsidy). We offer easy EMI options.

Do solar panels work in rainy season? expand_more

Yes! Panels generate power from light, not heat. While generation is lower on cloudy days, modern panels are highly efficient even in diffuse light.
