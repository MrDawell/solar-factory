Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about subsidies, installation, and bill savings? Find answers here.

How much subsidy can I get?
expand_more

Under the PM-Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, residential consumers in Indore can get up to ₹78,000 subsidy for a 3kW system. Our team handles 100% of the net-metering and subsidy applications.

What is the cost of installation?
expand_more

Costs vary based on system size and panel quality. Typically, a 3kW system starts from ₹1.5 Lakhs (before subsidy) in Madhya Pradesh. We offer easy zero-cost EMI options.

Do solar panels work in rainy season?
expand_more

Yes! Panels generate power from solar light, not heat. While generation is lower on heavy cloudy days, modern Tier-1 monocrystalline panels generate substantial power in the monsoon seasons of Madhya Pradesh.
